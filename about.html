About Me

I am a User Experience Specialist. I have an understanding of both the design needed to engage users as well the technical requirements needed for that engagement. I started out as a video editor when computers were just entering into the process. I have used Apple hardware most of my career and continue to do so. I spent over 15 years with Apple Inc. Over the course of my career I have implemented many IT solutions and have cultivated a good eye for design. User centered design is and always will be a passion of mine. I currently reside in the Palm Springs area of Southern Califonia. I have two puppies that I love taking for desert hikes. I also play golf as often as I can. I have been a fitness fanatic for many years and still lead an active lifestyle. Having traveled the world during my military service I have experienced many cultural differences and embrace those that make us understand others more easily.

User Empathy, Problem-Solving, Communication, Adaptability, Attention to Detail, Continuous Learning, Business Solutions, User Research, UX/UI Design, User Personas, User Testing, Wireframes, Prototyping, User Stories, Presentations, Marketing, Public Speaking.
