My work

Find Friends For

Directed a team of developers and designers to create this mobile app. Find Friends For is the complete platform for meeting new people and making friends through small group activities. The app allows for the creation, publishing, and joining of activities for small groups of people. Research found that making new friends for adults is difficult. Our app allows people to meet for activities and then form bonds that lead to friendships from those shared experiences.

View app Info https://findfriendsfor.com

FaceMod

FaceMod mobile app was developed to allow for the application of Effects makeup using Augmented Reality (AR). This design was created out of user desire to optimize the variety of make-up effects. The main areas that the mobile app addressed were being able to see what your face would look like prior to applying make up.

MTI Field Technicians Mobile App

This is a design for the MTI Field Technicians. MTI techs are dispatched to large retailers for installation of floor demonstration products. The field techs needed a replacement for the Salesforce mobile app as MTI had decided to not renew the Salesforce contract. This design was done as a POC to test against other solutions.
